Happy Valentine's Day to all! I made this webpage because I felt like it and I wondered whether I would be able to create little hearts. Also to hone my Javascript skills. Speaking of which, I have created a fun little game for you! Click the title for the menu of other games (WIP). Click the scoreboard if you don't like my colour scheme. Oh and by the way, DO NOT criticise my hearts. I literally have to have 3 elements in HTML and 12 seperate lines in CSS for EACH AND EVERY HEART. You do not want to know how much JavaScript it takes. And no I can not use an image because that would require me to send you the file as a zipped folder. Anyway, I will now give you the rules of the game.

Game Rules
1. Click on any heart to start the game.
2. Each time you click on a heart, you gain 1 score.
3. Once the game begins, you have 10 seconds until it ends.
4. Gain as much score as possible during the time.
5. Score is displayed on the top left and the timer is on the top right.
6. When you click a heart, one random heart will appear. All other hearts will disappear.
7. It is possible for the same heart to appear multiple times in a row.
8. The locations of the 6 hearts will not change.
9. Post your score on Discord along with a creative complaint about my webpage!
